# blockchain-scalability-lab
Researching and building scalable blockchain systems from first principles.

This repository combines:

- Research journals
- Concept notes
- Whitepaper and academic paper reviews
- Blockchain implementations from first principles
- Scalability experiments and benchmarks
- Architecture designs and proposals
- A living research paper

## Goal

To understand the fundamental scalability limitations of blockchain systems and explore architectures capable of achieving high throughput, low latency, and decentralization.

## Research Areas

- Consensus Mechanisms
- Parallel Execution
- State Management
- Block Propagation
- Data Structures
- Networking
- Sharding
- DAG-Based Systems
- Verkle Trees
- Zero-Knowledge Systems

## Repository Structure

roadmap/           → Learning roadmap and milestones

journal/           → Weekly research journals

concepts/          → Notes and explanations

papers/            → Whitepaper and paper summaries

implementations/   → Building blockchain components from scratch

experiments/       → Scalability and performance experiments

ideas/             → Research ideas and observations

architecture/      → Architecture drafts and proposals

research-paper/    → Living research paper

resources/         → Books, papers, courses, and references


## Philosophy

Learn → Build → Experiment → Question → Improve

Every concept studied is accompanied by notes, code, experiments, or observations.

This repository is intentionally public to document progress, mistakes, insights, and growth over time.
